Tadpole Hunting

Tadpoles wanted to find their mother. They went to Ms.fish, who told them that she's not their mother. They went to Ms. shrimp, she told them that she's not their mother. They went to Ms. Frog, who told them that she is their mother. At this happy family reunion time, the tadpoles were suddenly pulled up from the water by two human hands. (photo credit: my mom maybe?)
