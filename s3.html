Tadpole Hunting

The tadpoles were looking for their mother. They went to Ms.Fish, she told them that she's not their mother. They went to Ms. Shrimp, she told them that she's not their mother. They went to Ms. Frog, she told them that she is indeed their mother. At the time of this happy family reunion, two human hands reached into the water and took some tadpoles away.
